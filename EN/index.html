FAQ

Q1: Can it handle pigment inks?

Yes. We tune fiber compactness for pigment size/dispersion/viscosity to avoid clogging while keeping backflow.

Q2: How to reduce long-term leakage?

For solvent inks, increase film proportion, optimize end sealing, and enhance summer temperature control & packaging.

Q3: Very small OD possible?

We can go to Ø5 mm. We’ll co-evaluate barrel tolerance, inner wall friction, and tip structure to ensure flow stability.

Q4: MOQ & Lead Time?

Small pilot runs for standard sizes; mass-production lead time depends on materials and line schedule. Rush orders negotiable.
