FAQ

Q1: Can pigment inks work?

Yes. We tune fiber density and dispersion compatibility to avoid clogging while keeping return flow.

Q2: How to avoid leakage in long-term storage?

For solvent inks we increase film ratio and optimize end-sealing; summer temperature control and packaging help too.

Q3: Can you make very small OD?

Down to Ø5 mm is possible; we must also evaluate tube tolerances, inner wall friction and nib design for stability.

Q4: MOQ & lead time?

Standard sizes can be piloted in small batches; mass-production lead time depends on materials and line scheduling.
